Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CGPA calculation

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a measure of academic performance calculated by dividing the total grade points earned by the total credits attempted. It provides a standardized way to evaluate academic achievement across different courses and semesters.

How is CGPA calculated?

CGPA = Total Grade Points รท Total Credits. Each course contributes to the total based on its credit hours and the grade received. The formula ensures that courses with more credits have a greater impact on the overall CGPA.

Why do different universities have different grading systems?

Universities may use different grading scales based on their academic policies, accreditation requirements, or institutional preferences. Our calculator supports the most common grading systems used by Bangladeshi universities.

Can I include previous credits from another university?

Yes! You can add previous credits in the "Previous Credits" section. This is useful for transfer students or those who have completed courses at other institutions. Make sure to use the grading system of your current university for consistency.

What's the difference between General and Semester-wise calculation?

General: Calculate CGPA from individual courses. Semester-wise: Calculate overall CGPA from semester GPAs. Use General for course-by-course calculation, and Semester-wise for tracking progress across multiple semesters.

How accurate is the calculator?

Our calculator uses the official grading systems of each university and follows standard CGPA calculation methods. However, always verify results with your university's official records, as specific institutional policies may vary.

Can I download my calculation results?

Yes! After calculating your CGPA, you can download a professional PDF report that includes all your course details, calculation breakdown, and university information. This is perfect for academic records or applications.
