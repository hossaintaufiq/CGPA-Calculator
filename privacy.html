Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the CGPA Calculator.

What Data We Collect

  • No personal data is required to use this tool.
  • We do not store your grades, credits, or calculation results.
  • Anonymous analytics may be used to improve the app experience.

How We Use Data

  • Data entered is processed only in your browser for calculation purposes.
  • We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

Data Security

We use modern web technologies and best practices to keep your data secure. Since no personal data is stored, your privacy is always protected.

Your Rights

  • You have full control over your data. Refreshing or closing the page erases all entered information.
  • If you have questions, you can contact us below.

Contact Info

For privacy-related questions, please email support@cgpa-calculator.com.
